1

For Tablet Installation

Copy the Kiosk Interface link and paste it into your tablet's browser. Set it to full-screen kiosk mode for customers to provide feedback.

2

Share Dashboard with Management

Download or print the Dashboard QR Code and share it with GM, IT Department, and Head Managers. They can scan it with their phones to access real-time analytics anytime, anywhere.

Use the "Copy Link" button to copy the dashboard URL, then send it via email or WhatsApp to stakeholders. They can bookmark it for quick access.

4

Print the QR codes and display them in manager offices, meeting rooms, or staff areas. Anyone can scan to view current feedback status.

5

View Reports Automatically

Dashboard updates automatically every 30 seconds. All stakeholders see the same real-time data. Use the email reporting feature to send scheduled reports to all recipients.
